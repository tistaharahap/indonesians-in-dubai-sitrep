## Situation Summary

**Day four of the US-Israel vs Iran war — escalation continues to peak with no signs of de-escalation.** The US and Israel are conducting **non-stop airstrikes on Tehran**, including the **targeted killing of a senior Iranian commander** and the **bombing of the Natanz nuclear enrichment facility** (confirmed by IAEA). Trump stated the **"big wave" of attacks has not yet come** and the war could last **4-5 weeks**. Iran has **rejected all negotiations** and retaliated by hitting the **US Embassy in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia** with drones — Saudi Arabia strongly condemned the attack.

**Casualties in Iran: 787 killed**, hundreds injured (Red Crescent). Iran has launched strikes across **9 countries** including the UAE: Bahrain, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and a UK base in Cyprus. Israel also **struck the Iranian presidential office in Tehran** and **destroyed Iran's state TV/radio headquarters**.

**In the UAE:** The situation remains critical. Emirates has **suspended ALL flights until March 4 23:59 UAE time** — only limited repatriation and cargo flights. Etihad issued a **"don't come to the airport" advisory** without booking confirmation. **DXB and DWC operating at limited capacity** after a 48-hour total shutdown. **Strait of Hormuz remains closed** — 750 ships stuck, 10% of the global container fleet held up. **UAE stock exchanges closed.** Previous strikes on **Sharjah, Fujairah, and Abu Dhabi** confirm the UAE as an active target.

**Russia warns**: this war could push Iran and Arab nations to **seek nuclear weapons**.
