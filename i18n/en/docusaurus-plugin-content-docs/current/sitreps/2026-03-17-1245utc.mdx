Iranian drone hit a fuel tank near Dubai International Airport on March 16. Fire broke out, DXB fully closed. Dubai Civil Defence contained the fire (no casualties). Surrounding roads — airport road, tunnel, Al Garhoud Bridge, Cargo Village — closed by police.

- **Shah Gas Field, Abu Dhabi** — drone strike, fires
- **DXB fuel tank** — airport temporarily paralyzed

### Iran FM Rejects Ceasefire — "No Negotiations with US"

Iran FM Araghchi: Tehran seeks a **"decisive outcome"**, NOT a ceasefire. Rejects negotiations with the US. This means the war will continue INDEFINITELY.

### NATO Rejects Hormuz Coalition — "Not Our War"

Germany explicitly rejected: **"not our war."** Australia also declined warships. Only Turkey managed to pass ONE ship with Iranian approval. 500 tankers stuck at Hormuz. Trump's coalition has failed to materialize.
