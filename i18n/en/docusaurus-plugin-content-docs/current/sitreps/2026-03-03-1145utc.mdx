**Day six of the US-Israel vs Iran war — UAE holding under attack, partial stabilization signals emerging.** Iran continues launching missiles and drones at the UAE — **a drone strike hit a fuel facility in Abu Dhabi**, sparking a fire. - Limited repatriation flights **MAY NOT accept animals** in cabin/cargo

- Identify **emergency pet boarding/shelter** as a last-resort option
