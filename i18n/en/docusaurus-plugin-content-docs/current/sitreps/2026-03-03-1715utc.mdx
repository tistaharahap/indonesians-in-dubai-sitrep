## 5) Recommended Actions

### For ALL households:

1. **DO NOT go to the airport** without direct airline confirmation. Flights are extremely limited.
2. **Prepare go-bags** with essential documents (passport, visa, ID, vaccination records, cash).
3. **Identify the safest room** in your home (interior, away from windows/glass).
4. **Stock water and food** for at least 3-5 days.
5. **Charge all devices**, prepare power banks.
6. **Register with KBRI/KJRI** if you haven't: WA +971 565115193 (KBRI Abu Dhabi).
7. **Avoid areas near military facilities** — Al Minhad, US bases.

### Households with CHILDREN:

- Prepare children's documents (passport, birth certificate).
- Identify child-safe evacuation routes.
- Repatriation flights are VERY limited — **prioritise children if slots available**.
- Prepare offline entertainment for children.

### Households with PETS:

- Ensure carriers are ready; vaccination & pet documents complete.
- **WARNING**: Repatriation flights most likely will NOT accept pets. Options are very limited.
- Identify friends/acquaintances who could care for pets in an emergency evacuation.
- This is not the time to assume this problem can be solved later.

### Households with ELDERLY/MOBILITY CONSTRAINTS:

- Ensure medication supply for at least 2 weeks.
- Identify nearest hospital and fastest route.
- If special medical needs exist, report to KBRI for evacuation priority.

⚠️ **OPINION SECTION** — The following is analytical reasoning based on available information at the time of writing. It is not fact, not conclusive, and represents one possible interpretation. All posture claims are grounded in the factual developments reported above.

- **US destroyed Assembly of Experts building in Qom**: This may signal Washington's intent to prevent an organized leadership succession post-Khamenei — targeting not just military but political command chains. The message: there will be no orderly transition.

- **Trump: "4-5 weeks"**: Calibrating US domestic expectations. But military analysts (Michael Clarke, Sky News) assess Iran is the one prolonging conflict through regional escalation — Iran's strategy is to hurt the Gulf enough to pressure America. Iran "can't win militarily and they know it" but is hoping to "get lucky."

- **Iran rejects negotiations**: Signals to domestic audiences and proxies that resistance continues despite "degraded" capabilities (NATO's word). Also a message to the IRGC rank-and-file: no surrender.

- **Hezbollah declares open war**: Escalatory rhetoric backed by 9 attacks today. Likely signaling to Tehran that a second front remains active, but Hezbollah is materially weaker post-2024.

- **Qatar, Saudi emphasize "not part of the campaign"**: Gulf states signal to Tehran they are not aggressors, only defending themselves — trying to minimize further Iranian retaliation against their territory.
