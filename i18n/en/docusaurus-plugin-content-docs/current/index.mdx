## What Is This?

This site provides structured situation reports (SITREPs) for **Indonesian nationals living in Dubai and the UAE** during periods of regional instability. Each report includes:

- **Verified developments** from multiple independent sources
- **Direct threat assessment** to Dubai and the UAE
- **Indonesian diplomatic status** from KBRI Abu Dhabi, KJRI Dubai, and KBRI Riyadh
- **Actionable recommendations** including exit routes that account for families with pets
- **DEFCON-style threat level** (4 = Elevated → 1 = Evacuate)
