## 5) Recommended Actions / Rekomendasi Tindakan

### All Households
- **SHELTER-FIRST**: Stay indoors. Drone strikes confirmed hitting facilities in Dubai — the risk is real.
- **DO NOT go to airport** without direct airline confirmation your flight is departing.
- **Cash + documents**: Passport, visa, family docs, cash in one grab bag.
- **Resupply**: Water, non-perishable food, medications for at least 72 hours.

### Households with Pets 🐕
- Carrier ready + vaccination papers + health certificate. Many evacuation routes reject undocumented animals.
- If air evacuation: confirm airline pet policy BEFORE going to airport.
- Land evacuation to Oman more flexible for pets.

### Households with Children 👶
- 72-hour go-bag: fever meds, milk/snacks, child ID documents, small toy.
- Ensure children know their full name + parent's phone number.

### Medical/Mobility Needs ♿
- Stock at least 2 weeks of medication.
- Identify non-air backup transport routes.
- Coordinate with KBRI if special evacuation assistance needed.
