## How DEFCON Levels Are Assigned

Each SITREP assigns exactly **one DEFCON level** based on the highest applicable criteria. When evidence is ambiguous but suggests escalation, we **round UP** — false alarms are better than missed warnings.

## Level 4 — Elevated Awareness 🔵

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Criteria" icon="list-check">
    - Active military conflict in the region but NOT directly involving UAE
    - Strikes limited to Iran, Israel, Iraq, Syria, or Yemen with no spillover
    - UAE airspace open and operating normally
    - No credible threats to UAE territory
    - Strait of Hormuz open for navigation
    - Indonesian embassy operating normally, no advisories issued
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recommended Actions" icon="clipboard-list">
    - Monitor situation every 4-6 hours
    - Ensure passports and key documents are accessible
    - Keep phone charged, emergency contacts saved
    - Note current flight availability to Jakarta/Bali as baseline
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Level 3 — High Alert 🟡

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Criteria" icon="list-check">
    - Strikes or missile/drone activity within 500km of UAE borders
    - Houthi or Iranian-aligned attacks on Gulf shipping or nearby states
    - Partial airspace restrictions in the Gulf region
    - Credible threats suggesting UAE could be targeted
    - Indonesian embassy issues travel advisory or heightened alert
    - Disruption to Strait of Hormuz traffic
    - Significant escalation rhetoric from Iran toward Gulf states
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recommended Actions" icon="clipboard-list">
    - Monitor situation every 1-2 hours
    - [Pack emergency go-bags](/emergency/go-bag-checklist) for all family members
    - Research live flight availability: DXB → CGK, DXB → DPS, DXB → KUL, DXB → SIN
    - Identify pet-friendly airlines with current availability
    - Contact KJRI Dubai and KBRI Abu Dhabi for latest guidance
    - Top up vehicle fuel to full
    - Withdraw emergency cash (USD preferred, AED secondary)
    - Identify shelter locations (interior rooms, underground parking)
    - Share location and plans with trusted contacts in Indonesia
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Level 2 — Imminent Threat 🟠

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Criteria" icon="list-check">
    - Direct strikes on UAE territory or interceptions over UAE airspace
    - UAE air defense activations confirmed
    - Dubai or Abu Dhabi airports partially or fully suspended
    - Iranian or proxy forces explicitly targeting UAE
    - Mass evacuations beginning in neighboring countries
    - Indonesian embassy activates emergency protocols or issues evacuation advisory
    - Strait of Hormuz closed or severely restricted
    - Multiple Gulf state airspaces closed simultaneously
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recommended Actions" icon="clipboard-list">
    - Monitor **CONTINUOUSLY** (every 15-30 minutes)
    - **ACTIVATE EXIT PLANS** — bags at the door, family ready to move within 30 minutes
    - Execute the best available [exit route](/emergency/exit-routes)
    - Register with Indonesian embassy emergency system if not already done
    - Coordinate with other Indonesian nationals for group evacuation if possible
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Level 1 — Evacuate 🔴

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Criteria" icon="list-check">
    - Active bombardment of UAE territory
    - Nuclear, chemical, or biological threat to the Gulf region
    - Complete collapse of civil infrastructure (airports, ports, communications)
    - UAE government orders evacuation of civilians
    - Indonesian government initiates formal evacuation of nationals
    - Imminent ground conflict reaching UAE borders
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recommended Actions" icon="clipboard-list">
    - **EXECUTE BEST AVAILABLE EXIT ROUTE IMMEDIATELY**
    - Do NOT wait for Indonesian embassy evacuation — it may be slow, limited, and may not accommodate pets
    - Prioritize: Air → Land (Oman) → Sea (Fujairah) → Extended Land
    - If all routes blocked: **shelter in place** in most protected structure (underground parking, interior rooms)
    - Gather water, food, first aid supplies
    - Maintain communication with KJRI Dubai, KBRI Abu Dhabi, and family
    - Broadcast location to family and emergency contacts every 2 hours
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
